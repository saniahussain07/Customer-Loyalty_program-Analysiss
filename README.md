# Customer Loyalty Program Analysis 📊
## Overview
Business Intelligence Dashboard analyzing 84,437 rows of Customer Loyalty Program data using Microsoft Excel.
## Key Findings
🎮 TV & Video Gaming drives 44% of total revenue
🌍 US leads revenue but all 4 countries within 3% of each other
🥇 Bronze members generate highest revenue (25%)
💳 100% of purchases are coupon-driven
👥 Perfect 50/50 gender split in spending
## Tools Used
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Charts & Dashboard
## Dataset
84,437 rows
4 countries (USA, Canada, UK, Germany)
6 loyalty tiers
5 product lines
